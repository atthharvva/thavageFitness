At Thavage Fitness, we offer a wide range of facilities to help you achieve your fitness goals.






Cardio Machines

  • Rowing Machines







Weight Machines

Our weight machines target all major muscle
groups and are suitable for both

your fitness goals.

Relax and unwind after your workout with our
sauna and steam rooms.
